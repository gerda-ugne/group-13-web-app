About the store

The Little Green Larder is a zero waste shop in Dundee, Scotland.

At The Little Green Larder we have a large variety of eco friendly alternatives to every day items. We have metal straws, reusable water bottles, reusable sanitary items, soap bars, soy wax wraps, dish brushes, as well as fresh fruit and veg, bread, whole foods, fried foods and refillable oil, vinegar and cleaning liquids.

Pop in to see us to find out more!
