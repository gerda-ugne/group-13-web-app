Give boxes

Got any unwanted items? Take any reusable items going to waste to the locations displayed on the map and they'll be used to help local people in need. Donate your old clothes, shoes and household goods. These places will accept: old clothes, shoes accessories, crockery, cutlery, pots & pans, utensils,bedding, curtains, towels, lamps, kettles, toasters, irons, hoovers etc. Even if the charity shops won't take it, drop it off in the box and they'll do their best to get it reused. See the map below to find your nearest box.
