Track your trash!

Carbon footprint calculator is a tool that will aid you in reducing your overall waste.

Input the amount of the type of waste you have into the fields below. Each type will give a score depending upon how easily recyclable your waste is. The lower the score, the better!

Glass bottles
Cardboard boxes
Tins
Plastic bottles and punets
Drink Cartons
Plastic bags
Plastic film
Soiled cardboard boxes
Crisp packet

Your score is:

  • Grade A = 0 points or less

    You're doing great, keep it up!

  • Grade B = 1 > 10 points

    You should look into reducing your waste.

  • Grade C = 10>20 points

    Your waste is on unacceptable levels.

  • Grade D = 30 points or greater

    You must reduce your waste. You're killing the planet!
