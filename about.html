What is zero waste?

Zero waste is exactly what it sounds like: wasting nothing! Right now, companies all over the company extract resources from the planet and dump them in a landfill. This in turn creates serious issues for the plantes condition. Zero waste is a strategy to prevent this by reducing the amount of materials that are harmful to the environment. The overall goal in zero waste is to eliminate all waste on the planet.

Why should you go zero waste?

There are many reasons you should go zero waste. Not only does it help the planet but is also good for the community. More than £24 billion of food is wasted every year which be resolved by redistributing unwanted food to shelters, food banks and more. The economy is also improved since recycling and reuse create at least nine times more jobs than landfills and incinerators. Most importantly, our planet is dying. Waste and pollution are causing serious changes in our climate. What we need to do now more than ever is to take action to prevent this or there will be serious consequences in the future.

Where should you start?

There are many small changes you can make in your life that can really make a difference. Just simple things like reusing bottles, switching the tv off when no ones using it, buying less meat. A good idea would be to start recycling as well. Keep seperate bins for different materials like food, glass and plastic. That way, you can help the environment through eliminating waste. Have a look through the Advice page to get a better understanding of what you can do.
