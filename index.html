About the store

The Little Green Larder is a zero waste shop in Dundee, Scotland.

At The Little Green Larder we have a large variety of eco friendly alternatives to every day items. We have metal straws, reusable water bottles, reusable sanitary items, soap bars, soy wax wraps, dish brushes and much more.

Pop in to see us to find out more!

Jillian, The Little Green Blogger - owner of the store
