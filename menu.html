Our Products

Welcome to the Products page of the website. Here you can have a look at the variety of different products you are likely to see on your next trip to The Little Green Larder. All our products are kept in biodegradable packaging or in reusable containers.

We try our best to tailor to most if not all of our customers as we know there are some of you out there that have chose a specific diet or are restricted to a diet due to allergies or health.

If you have any questions about our products or if you wish for something to become an addition, then come on in for a chat and I'm sure we can find what you need.
